What is a Elixir?

elixir is A pharmaceutical preparation with an ingredient (properties like that of morphine solution) is a solution that is a highly active substance of ethyl alcohol. It must be taken orally. They are sugared or glycerin, containing aromatic substances and as drug bases.

The elixir initially appeared in times of high ancestral degree, and in the medieval period it became popular. There are different types of elixir, to cure eyesight, improve blood circulation, cure ailments and other period ailments. In some cases they were even used for more fanciful, mystical and allegorical matters.
